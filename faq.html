Frequently Asked Questions

Q: If I ship will the cost be included in the services?

A: The amount for return shipping will be stated and added to your order.

 

Q: How much does it cost?

A: Our minimum for customs is $200, but can vary depending on the design.

 

Q: How long until I get my shoes back?

A: They can take up to 2-3 weeks depending on volume of orders and design of the shoe.
